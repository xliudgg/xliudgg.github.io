G-WHISPER: GAN WITH HIERARCHICAL VAE FOR SPEECH RECONSTRUCTION

Xueling Liu, Yu Gao, Jiancheng Ye

AI Innovation Center, Midea Group (Shanghai) Co.,Ltd., China

Abstract

Several GAN-based vocoders have achieved success in fast and high-fidelity speech generation. To enhance speech quality, many of these models focus on adding various multi-resolution discriminators on time and frequency domains. In this paper, we propose G-WHISPER, which introduces deep hierarchical VAE to waveform generation by extracting latent variables from original waveforms. Meanwhile, we preserve the generative adversarial structure and adopt multi-resolution spectrogram discriminators proposed by previous GAN-based models. Experimental results show it is possible to combine deep hierarchical VAE and GAN to achieve fast and high-fidelity waveform reconstruction with a small number of parameters (4.14M). Mean Opinion Score (MOS) results show our model is highly competitive among models evaluated in experiments, in both seen and unseen domains. Furthermore, we measured inference speeds and demonstrated the real-time speech synthesis capacity of our model.
