NVAEGAN-Vocoder: A Waveform Generation Model Based on Deep Hierarchical VAE

Xueling Liu1, Yu Gao1, Jiancheng Ye1
1 AI Innovation Center, Midea Group (Shanghai) Co.,Ltd., China

Abstract

Several GAN-based vocoders has achieved success in fast parallel speech generation. Most of these models focus on adding or modifying multi-resolution discriminators on time and frequency domain to enhance speech quality. In this paper, we propose NVAEGAN-Vocoder, a neural vocoder which can synthesize high-quality speech from mel-spectrograms. We introduce deep hierarchical VAE to vocoders and extract latent variables from original waveforms. Meanwhile, in this work, we preserve the generative and adversarial structure and directly adopt spectrogram discriminator proposed by previous GAN-based model without modification. Experimental results show that it is possible to combine deep hierarchical VAE and GAN to achieve fast high-quality waveform reconstruction with small number of parameters (4.13M). Mean Opinion Score(MOS) of our proposed model outperforms other competing models in both seen domain and unseen domain. Furthermore, we measured inference speed of NVAEGAN-Vocoder and demonstrated the real-time speech synthesis capacity of our model.
